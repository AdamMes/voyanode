About VoyaNode

A Next-Generation General AI Travel Advisor

Our Mission

VoyaNode is designed to be a completely flexible AI Travel Advisor. Unlike standard bots with fixed knowledge, VoyaNode is an empty canvas that learns from the documents you provide.

Whether it's a PDF about a boutique hotel in Paris or a CSV of restaurant reviews in Tokyo, VoyaNode processes this information using Retrieval-Augmented Generation (RAG) to provide factual, source-backed answers.

The RAG Pipeline

  • 1. Ingestion: Files are uploaded to S3 and tracked by SQS.
  • 2. Processing: Documents are cleaned and split into semantic chunks.
  • 3. Vectorization: Text is converted into high-dimensional vectors via Titan v2.
  • 4. Retrieval: When you ask a question, we find the most relevant chunks in OpenSearch.
  • 5. Generation: Claude 3.5 synthesizes the final answer using only the retrieved context.
